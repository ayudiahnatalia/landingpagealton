Absolutely not. Everything you share with me is completely confidential. I take this more seriously than you can imagine — because I know what it cost me to share my own story. Your secret is safe. Your healing is between you, me, and God.
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
Send me a message first. We talk. If I don't genuinely believe I can help you, I'll tell you — and I'll point you to someone who can. I will never take your money for a problem I'm not equipped to help with. That's the commitment.
